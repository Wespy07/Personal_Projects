Bubble Trouble

    How to play:

  • Click on start game to begin.
  • A random number between 1 and 10 will be displayed as your Hit number.
  • Bubbles numbered between 1 and 10 will appear on the screen randomly.
  • Click on the bubble that matches the hit number to score points.
  • Each correct match adds 10 points to your score.
  • You have 60 seconds to score as many points as possible.
  • There's a surprise element to this as well
